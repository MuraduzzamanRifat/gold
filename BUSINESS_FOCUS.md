# Gold Kings Business Focus & Content Guidelines

## ✅ What Gold Kings Buys (Focus Items)

### Primary Categories

#### 1. Gold Jewelry
- Gold chains (14K, 18K, 22K)
- Gold rings (engagement, wedding, vintage)
- Gold bracelets & bangles
- Gold watches (vintage & modern)
- Gold necklaces & pendants
- Gold earrings
- Gold dental work / scrap gold
- Gold plated items with gold content

#### 2. Gold Coins & Bullion
- American Gold Eagles
- Canadian Gold Maple Leafs
- South African Krugerrands
- Vintage gold coins (pre-1933)
- Commemorative gold coins
- Gold bars & ingots
- Gold bullion coins

#### 3. Silver Items
- Sterling silver jewelry
- Silver coins (dimes, quarters, dollars)
- Silver bullion coins
- Silver bars & ingots
- Silver tableware & flatware
- Vintage silver items
- Silver scrap

#### 4. Precious Metals & Vintage Items
- Platinum jewelry
- Vintage watches (gold/silver cases)
- Antique jewelry with precious metals
- Estate jewelry collections
- Heirloom pieces
- Vintage brooches & pins (with gold/silver)
- Scrap precious metals

## ❌ What Gold Kings Does NOT Buy

### Gaming & Non-Precious Items
- ❌ Gaming collectibles (cards, figurines)
- ❌ Pokemon cards or gaming merchandise
- ❌ Action figures or toys
- ❌ Video game consoles
- ❌ Memorabilia or sports cards
- ❌ Comic books

### Fashion & Costume Jewelry
- ❌ Costume jewelry (no precious metals)
- ❌ Fashion jewelry with no gold/silver content
- ❌ Plated items without base metal value
- ❌ Stainless steel items (non-precious)
- ❌ Cheap metals or alloys

### Other Items
- ❌ Electronics (except if containing precious metals)
- ❌ Books or documents
- ❌ Clothing or accessories
- ❌ Household items
- ❌ Furniture
- ❌ Vintage cars or large items
- ❌ Cryptocurrency or digital items

## Team Communication

### Internal Guidelines
1. **Always specify** "gold & silver" or "precious metals"
2. **Never accept** gaming items, collectibles, or costume jewelry
3. **Emphasize** fair pricing, instant cash, expertise in precious metals
4. **Decline politely** if customer has non-precious items
5. **Refer** interested customers to appropriate buyers:
   - Gaming collectibles → Gaming shops
   - Memorabilia → Antique dealers
   - Costume jewelry → Thrift stores

```

We specialize in buying gold, silver, and
```
