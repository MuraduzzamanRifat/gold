For the seventeen years we've held a license, the deal hasn't changed. You walk in with metal — broken chain, grandmother's coins, a watch you never wore — and we test it on the counter, in front of you. We weigh it on a calibrated scale you can read. We do the math out loud, against the published spot price for the day.

The number we write down is the number you walk out with. No quotes-by-phone, no mailing-it-in, no surprise deductions. Cash, check, Zelle, CashApp, Venmo, PayPal, or wire — your choice, settled the same day.

We pay seventy to ninety percent of spot, depending on karat and current refining premiums. On certified numismatic coins and brand-name luxury watches, we go beyond melt value when condition and provenance support it.

Two doors. Same handshake. Mondays through Saturdays, ten to six. The metal is worth what it's worth — our job is to be honest about it, and quick.
