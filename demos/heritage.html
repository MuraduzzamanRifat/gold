Chapter II — Our Heritage

A family establishment, since 2008.

Adam founded Gold Kings out of a single Snellville storefront with a simple promise: weigh in front of the customer, calculate against the live spot price, settle the same day, in full. Three years later, the second location opened in Commerce. The promise hasn't changed.

"There's no trick. The metal is worth what it's worth — our job is to be honest about it, and quick."

— Adam, Proprietor
