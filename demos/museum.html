— Curators' Note

Why we weigh
in front of you.

Every gold buyer in the country quotes the same spot price. The difference between honest dealers and the rest is what happens between quote and cash.

We test in front of you. We weigh on a calibrated scale you can read. We do the math out loud, against the published live price for the day. The number we write down is the number you walk out with.

"The metal is worth what it's worth. Our job is to be honest about it, and quick."

No quotes-by-phone. No mailing-it-in. No surprise deductions. Walk in with what you have, walk out with cash — usually inside ten minutes.
