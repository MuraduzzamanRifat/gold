◆ Church Gold Fundraising Program

Stewardship,
handled with care.

Help your congregation turn unused gold and silver into real funding for ministry — through a transparent process you can show your members.

For congregations across Georgia · Confidential evaluations

Many families have heirloom jewelry, coins, or silver tucked away that they'd happily donate to support their church — if they trusted the process. Gold Kings partners with churches to make that easy, dignified, and fully transparent.

Items your members can donate

  • Broken or outdated jewelry
  • Inherited gold or silver pieces
  • Rings, chains, bracelets, watches
  • Gold and silver coins
  • Anything specifically designated for the fundraiser

How the program works

01 · Voluntary collection

Members donate items willingly during a designated collection window. The decision is theirs, motivated by purpose — never obligation.

02 · Transparent evaluation

We come to you, or your designated team brings the items to one of our counters. Either way, every piece is tested and weighed openly using current market rates. Nothing happens behind the scenes.

03 · Fair market pricing

Offers reflect real-time precious-metal values with the calculation explained line by line. Your team can verify the math.

04 · Cash offer

The church receives proceeds with no rushed timeline. Take time to review, ask questions, and consult before accepting.

05 · Real impact

Funds go directly to the ministry, mission, or cause your church has selected. We provide a clean record so the congregation can see exactly what was raised.

Why churches partner with us

  • Respectful handling of donated materials
  • Transparent deductions with full explanations
  • No high-pressure sales tactics
  • Professional, documented assessments
  • Established relationships with faith organizations across Georgia

Stewardship with integrity

We treat donated items the way they were given — with care for the people who gave them. The goal is to honor that generosity with a process that's straightforward, ethical, and worthy of the trust your congregation has placed in your leadership.

Reserve an evaluation

To set up a church fundraising evaluation, call either Georgia counter and ask for the program coordinator. We'll discuss your church name, pastor or coordinator details, location, and the cause you're raising for, and arrange a time that works for your congregation.
