Effective Date · January 1, 2026

Gold Kings ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you visit our website, contact us, or visit our Snellville and Commerce, Georgia locations.

By using our website or our services, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Personal information

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • City or general location
  • Anything else you choose to share through a contact form or inquiry

Transaction & service information

  • Details about evaluations or inquiries (gold, silver, or other precious metals)
  • Records of communications (calls, emails, messages)
  • Government-issued ID details where required by Georgia law for in-person transactions

Website usage information

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Pages visited
  • Date and time of visits

This is used to keep the site working well and to understand which pages are useful.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information collected to:

  • Respond to inquiries and customer requests
  • Provide evaluations and customer support
  • Communicate about our services, hours, or location details
  • Improve website functionality and service quality
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to analyze traffic, understand visitor behavior, and improve site functionality. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work as well.

4. Information Sharing

We share information only where necessary:

  • To comply with legal requirements or law-enforcement requests
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With trusted service providers who help us operate the website (under confidentiality)

We never share personal data for marketing purposes without your consent.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. No online system is 100% secure, but we treat your data as if it were our own.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to third-party sites. Gold Kings is not responsible for those sites' privacy practices or content. Review their privacy policies before sharing information with them.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

8. Your Privacy Rights

You may:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections or updates
  • Request deletion (subject to our legal obligations as a licensed dealer)

To make a request, contact us using the details below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Please check back periodically.
