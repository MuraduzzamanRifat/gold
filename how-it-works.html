◆ How Selling Your Gold Works

Five steps,
about ten minutes.

Walk in. Watch us test and weigh. Hear how the offer is calculated. Decide. That's the whole process.

No appointment · Walk-ins welcome

Selling precious metal shouldn't be a guessing game. Here's exactly what happens when you bring something to a Gold Kings counter.

Step 01 · Bring your items in

Bring any gold, silver, coins, jewelry, or bullion to either Georgia counter for evaluation. Items can be broken, damaged, outdated, or mismatched — none of that affects the metal weight value. No appointment is needed; walk-ins are always welcome during business hours.

Step 02 · We test & weigh in front of you

Items stay visible on the counter the entire time. We use professional testing equipment to verify purity (karat for gold, fineness for silver) and a calibrated digital scale for weight. You can ask questions at any point — we'll explain anything you're not sure about.

Step 03 · We show you the live market price

Gold and silver prices change throughout the day. We pull the current spot rate at the moment of evaluation and apply it to the metal we measured. No fixed charts, no outdated rates — the number reflects the actual market that day.

Step 04 · You receive a clear cash offer

The offer is one number. No hidden fees, no surprise deductions, no confusing line items. We walk you through how the offer was calculated — purity × weight × current spot price × payout percentage — and answer any question.

Step 05 · You decide — no pressure

Accept the offer and we settle on the spot — cash, check, Zelle, CashApp, Venmo, PayPal, or wire. Your choice. Or take your items home and think it over. We never push.

Why we work this way

The whole process is built on three things: transparency at every step, open communication instead of jargon, and the freedom to make an informed decision without anyone breathing down your neck.

Want to see it in person? Both counters are open Mon–Sat, 10 AM – 6 PM.
