Plenty of places will buy your gold. We work hard to be the place you'd recommend to your mother. Here's how we do that.

01 · Transparent testing

Your items never leave your sight. Testing and weighing happen openly on the counter using professional equipment. Watch the test happen. Read the scale. Ask any question at any stage. We'd rather take an extra minute to explain than have you wondering what just happened.

02 · No-pressure environment

Every evaluation is free and carries no obligation. You can decline an offer and walk out with your items, no awkwardness, no guilt-trip, no follow-up call. The right answer is whatever's right for you.

03 · Immediate payment

If you accept the offer, you're paid on the spot. No waiting period. No "come back tomorrow." No follow-up appointment. Cash, check, Zelle, CashApp, Venmo, PayPal, or wire — your choice.

04 · Licensed local operation

We're a licensed Georgia precious-metals dealer operating two physical counters in Snellville and Commerce. Our license, our reputation, and our two doors all depend on treating people the way we'd want to be treated.

05 · Better than a pawn shop

We're not a pawn shop. Pawns make money on loan margins and resale markups, which means lower payouts to you. We specialize in metal evaluation against live spot — that's the entire business — which usually translates to a better number on your side of the counter.

06 · Professional experience

Clear communication. Fair, market-based pricing. Respect for your time and the items you're bringing in. A calm, professional setting. The whole point is to build the kind of trust that makes you happy you came — and ready to come back.

What customers actually say

Most of our business comes from repeat customers and referrals. People bring their gold once, see how the process works, then bring the next thing they have. That's the test we care about.
