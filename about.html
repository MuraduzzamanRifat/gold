Selling gold and silver should be honest, transparent, and fair. That's the principle the business was built on. Two Georgia counters, one promise.

Established · Snellville & Commerce, Georgia

Gold Kings was started to fix something simple. Too many people were leaving gold buyers feeling like they'd been talked past, watched at a distance while their items were tested, and pushed to decide quickly. We thought there was a better way.

Our promise

The business runs on three commitments — the same three, every visit, every customer:

  • Pricing is anchored to live spot value, with the math shown.
  • Testing and weighing happen in front of you, on professional equipment, with explanations in plain language.
  • You're never pressured to sell. Take time. Bring a friend. Walk out with your items if the offer isn't right.

How we work

We're not chasing fast turnover. We'd rather build a relationship that brings you back next time you have something to sell — or sends a friend our way.

That's why most of our business comes from repeat customers and referrals. We've earned that by treating every visit the same, regardless of how big or small the transaction is.

Where we are

We operate two physical counters in Georgia:

  • Snellville, GA — 3635 Stone Mountain Hwy. Inside Unique Treasures Antique & Flea Market. 770-771-4650
  • Commerce, GA — 480 Banks Crossing Dr. (706) 336-0043

Both are open Monday through Saturday, 10 AM to 6 PM. Walk-ins always welcome — no appointment needed.

What sets us apart

We're a precious-metals dealer, not a pawn shop. That means our entire business is built around evaluating metal accurately and paying live spot rates — not loaning against items or marking up for resale. The model is simpler, and so is the price.
