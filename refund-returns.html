Gold Kings is a precious-metals dealer, not a pawn shop. We do not issue loans, hold collateral, or offer item redemption.

Once a sale is complete, it's complete

When you sell an item to us, ownership transfers immediately and the transaction is final. For this reason, we do not offer:

  • Returns
  • Refunds
  • Exchanges
  • Repurchase guarantees
  • Buy-backs

Why this matters

We carefully test, weigh, and evaluate every item in front of you before any payment is made. You see what we see. You hear how the offer is calculated. You can ask any question, you can take time to think, and you can decline without obligation.

By accepting payment, you confirm that the sale is final.

Take your time

If you're unsure, please:

  • Ask us to explain the calculation again
  • Step away to think it over — we won't push you
  • Bring a friend or family member with you
  • Take the items home and come back another day

We'd rather you walk out with your items than complete a sale you regret.
